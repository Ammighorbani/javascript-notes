# Logical Operators

### "+":
 **To total variables**

### Example: ```alert(Num1 + Num2)```

### "-": 
 **To subtraction variables**

### Example: `alert(Num1 - Num2)`

### "*": 
 **To multiplication variables**

### Example: `alert(Num1 * Num2)`

### "/": 
 **To division variables**

### Example: `alert(Num1 / Num2)`

### "**":
 **To power variables**

### Example: `alert(Num1 ** Num2)`

### "%": 
 **to give left over of number**

### Example: `alert(Num1 % Num2)`
